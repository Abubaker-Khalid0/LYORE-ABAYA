Return & Exchange Policy

We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery. To be eligible, items must be unworn, unwashed, and include all original tags with the security seal intact.

How to Initiate a Return

1

Log in to your account or contact us via WhatsApp with your order ID.

2

Select the items you wish to return and specify the reason.

3

Our team will review your request and send a pre-paid shipping label within 24 hours.

4

Pack the items securely in the original packaging.

5

Drop off at your nearest collection point or schedule a courier pickup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I modify my order?

As we process orders quickly, modifications are only possible within 2 hours of placement. Please contact us via WhatsApp immediately.

Are bespoke orders eligible?

Custom-made or bespoke abayas are crafted specifically to your measurements and are therefore non-refundable unless a manufacturing defect exists.

How long does the refund take?

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 10-14 business days after we receive and inspect your returned items.

Who pays for return shipping?

For domestic orders over $500, return shipping is complimentary. For all other orders, a small processing fee is deducted from the refund.

Need Help?

Our concierge team is available to assist with your shipping or return queries.
